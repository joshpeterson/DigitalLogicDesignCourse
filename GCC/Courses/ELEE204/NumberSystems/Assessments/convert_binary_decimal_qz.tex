Convert the following binary numbers to decimal. \\
\\
$11110000_2$ \\
$01101100_2$ \\

%start answer
Solution: \\
\\
$11110000_2 = 1 \cdot 2^7 + 1 \cdot 2^6 + 1 \cdot 2^5 + 1 \cdot 2^4 = 128 + 64 + 32 + 16 = 240$ \\
$01101100_2 = 1 \cdot 2^6 + 1 \cdot 2^5 + 1 \cdot 2^3 + 1 \cdot 2^2 = 64 + 32 + 8 + 4 = 108$ \\
%end answer
