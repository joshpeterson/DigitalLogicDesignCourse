Convert the following binary numbers to decimal. \\
\\
$01001101_2$ \\
$1110.11_2$ \\

%start answer
Solution: \\
\\
$01001101_2 = 1 \cdot 2^6 + 1 \cdot 2^3 + 1 \cdot 2^2 + 1 \cdot 2^0 = 64 + 8 + 4 + 1 = 77$ \\
$1110.11_2 = 1 \cdot 2^3 + 1 \cdot 2^2 + 1 \cdot 2^1 + 1 \cdot 2^{-1} + 1 \cdot 2^{-2} = 8 + 4 + 2 + 0.5 + 0.25 = 14.75$ \\
%end answer
