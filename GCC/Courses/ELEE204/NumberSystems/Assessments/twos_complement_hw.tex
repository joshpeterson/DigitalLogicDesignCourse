Find the eight bit two's complement representation of each of the following numbers. \\
\\
$47$ \\
$-93$ \\

%start answer
Solution: \\
\\
$47 = 00101111_2$\\
\\
\begin{tabular}{rl}
  $93 =$ & $01011101_2$\\
         & $\Downarrow \textrm{complement}$\\
         & $10100010_2$\\
     $+$ & $00000001_2$\\
         & $10100011_2$\\
         & $= 1 \cdot -128 + 1 \cdot 32 + 1 \cdot 2 + 1 \cdot 1 = -93$
\end{tabular}\\
