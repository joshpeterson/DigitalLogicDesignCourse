Convert the following binary numbers to decimal. \\
\\
(a) $11100001_2$ \\
(b) $0001.101_2$ \\

%start answer
Solution: \\
\\
(a) $11100001_2 = 1 \cdot 2^7 + 1 \cdot 2^6 + 1 \cdot 2^5 + 1 \cdot 2^0 = 128 + 64 + 32 + 1 = 225$ \\
(b) $0001.101_2 = 1 \cdot 2^0 + 1 \cdot 2^{-1} + 1 \cdot 2^{-3} = 1 + 0.5 + 0.125 = 1.625$ \\
%end answer
